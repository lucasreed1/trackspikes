Track Spikes Protective Cover

Product Overview

Purpose: Protects track spikes from wear, dullness, dirt, and bag damage.

Functionality: Clips onto the bottom of spikes for easy attachment and removal.

Product Development & Innovation

Manufacturing Process: 3D printing, injection molding, thermoforming, die-cutting.

  • Stronger design while maintaining a snug fit for various spike types.
  • Additional clip for easy attachment to backpacks.
  • Minimal market competition (2-3 similar products exist).

Design & Specifications

  • Offset: 0.05 inches between protector and spike.
  • Dimensions: 2 inches outward, 1.9 inches high from toebox.
  • Materials: Initial prototype 3D printed; final material selection TBD.

Prototyping & Testing

Prototyped using additive manufacturing. Adjustments made based on feedback to improve durability and usability.

Design Process

  1. Design Brief: Develop a protective cover for track spikes.
  2. Generate Concepts: Explored clip-on and slip-on designs.
  3. Select Solution: Snug fit with an additional clip for portability.
  4. Prototype & Test: Built using 3D printing (MakerBot/Glowforge).
  5. Evaluation: Improved material strength and flexibility.
  6. Presentation: Created a 1-minute commercial for showcase.
